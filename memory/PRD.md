# Happy Place Coffee & Eats - Product Requirements Document

## Original Problem Statement
Build a beautiful redesigned landing page for Happy Place Coffee and Eats using:
- Their actual logo and images from their website (NO stock photos)
- The warm, family-owned local coffee shop vibe
- Professional Starbucks-like structure and functionality
- Emphasis on family-owned values, flavor, and customer experience

## What's Been Implemented (Updated - January 5, 2025)

### Brand Assets Integration
✅ **Authentic Branding**
- Using Happy Place's actual logo: `https://lirp.cdn-website.com/ee24b866/dms3rep/multi/opt/Happy-Logo-1920w.png`
- All images sourced from Happy Place website (no stock photos)
- Hero images: Coffee & pastries, shrimp quesadilla, coffee & bagel, desserts

### Pages & Content (All with Authentic Images)
1. **Home Page**
   - Hero: "Family-Owned Since Day One" badge
   - Messaging: "Every dish made with love, every guest treated like family"
   - Stats: Family Owned (Local), 50K+ Cups Served, 10K+ Happy Guests, 5+ Years
   - Features real images from their menu

2. **Menu Page**
   - Complete menu scraped from website with actual prices
   - Categories: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner, Coffee & Drinks, Desserts
   - Real menu items: Baleadas Catrachas ($10), Lomo Saltado ($23.95), Mix Fajitas ($31.99)
   - Add to cart functionality with mock data

3. **Other Pages**
   - Rewards: Loyalty program with 3 tiers
   - Blog: Coffee culture stories emphasizing family recipes
   - About: "Built on Family & Flavor" story
   - Locations: Woodbridge, VA location with Google Maps

### Design & Messaging Focus
✅ **Family-Owned Emphasis**
- "Family-Owned Since Day One" in hero

- Personal service and community focus

✅ **Flavor & Quality**

✅ **Customer Experience**

- Testimonials emphasize personal connections
- Community hub messaging
